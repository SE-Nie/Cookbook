Greek Salad

This is a great mediterranean salad to take to a barbeque. All ingredients are approximate, so add more or less of any ingredient depending on your own taste.

Greek Salad
Share

Ingredients

3 medium cucumbers
1 1/2 cups crumbled feta cheese
1 cup sliced black olives
3 cups fresh diced tomatos
1/3 cup oil packed sun-dried tomatos
1/2 sliced red onion

Instructions

  1. In a large salad bowl, toss together the cucumbers, feta cheese, olives, roma tomatoes, sun-dried tomatoes, 2 tablespoons reserved sun-dried tomato oil, and red onion. Chill until serving.
  2. Bon appetit!
